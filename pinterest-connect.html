How it works

Three steps to auto-publish your products

1

Connect your Pinterest

Click the button above. You'll be redirected to Pinterest to authorise LU2COHOUSE access via OAuth 2.0.

2

Select your board

After connecting, your Pinterest boards are retrieved via the API. Choose where new products should be pinned.

3

Products auto-post

New patterns are automatically published as Pins with the product image, title, description and link to your shop.

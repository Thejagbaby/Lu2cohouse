## How to Download (Etsy Policy — Instant Download)

Leila's shop offers **instant downloads only** (not made-to-order).

**After purchase:**
- Customer receives an email notification when files are ready
- Click "View your files on Etsy" in the email → goes to Purchases section of Etsy account
- In Etsy account: go to **Your account → Purchases → Download Files**
- Files can be downloaded unlimited times with no expiry (unless order is cancelled)
- If the Download Files button is gray, payment is still processing

**Important notes for customers:**
- Cannot download via the Etsy app — must use Etsy.com on mobile browser or computer
- If download email is missing: check spam/junk, Gmail Social/Promotions tabs
- Add transaction@account.etsy.com or noreply@etsy.com to safe sender list
- If files won't open: disable browser add-ons/ad blockers and retry, or re-download
- Guest purchases (no Etsy account): download link is in the receipt email sent after purchase

**Refunds / cancellations:**
- Digital listings cannot be returned or cancelled
- If item is not as described or file is never delivered, customer may be covered under Etsy's Purchase Protection

Instant Digital Download Policy

Everything you need to know about how your sewing pattern PDF is delivered, what's included, and how to use it — the same terms that apply on our Etsy shop, clearly laid out here.

Section 01

How your download works

Every LU2COHOUSE sewing pattern is an instant digital download. There is nothing to ship and no waiting time. As soon as your payment is confirmed on Etsy, your files are available immediately inside your Etsy account.

You can find your downloads at any time by going to Etsy → Account → Purchases and Reviews and clicking the download button next to your order. Files are available for download for the lifetime of your Etsy account.

You do not need to create an Etsy account to purchase, but having one ensures you can re-download your files at any time if the originals are lost or your device changes.

Section 02

What's included in every pattern

Each LU2COHOUSE listing includes a complete booklet-style PDF sewing pattern — all pattern pieces, step-by-step illustrated instructions, a size chart, and finished garment measurements. Every download comes in five formats so you can print however works best for you:

A4  (international) US Letter  (8.5 × 11 in) A0  (copyshop / plotter) Size-Layered PDF Sizes in cm & in

Print at home on A4 or US Letter, or take the A0 file to a copy shop for a single large-format sheet with no taping. The layered PDF lets you open the file in Adobe Acrobat and toggle individual size layers on or off — so you only print the sizes you need. All measurements are provided in both centimetres and inches throughout.

Seam allowances are included in every pattern. Skill level and recommended fabrics are listed on the product page before you purchase.

License & usage rights

Your purchase grants you a personal, non-transferable license to use this pattern. The following uses are permitted and prohibited:

  • You may print and use the pattern as many times as you need for your own personal sewing projects.
  • You may sell finished garments you make from our patterns — the pattern is yours to sew with.
  • You may not share, distribute, resell, or gift the PDF file itself to anyone else.
  • You may not upload the file to any file-sharing platform, cloud storage link, or social platform in any form.
  • You may not reproduce or repackage the pattern pieces or instructions as your own product.

If someone in your sewing circle wants the pattern, please direct them to our Etsy shop. Each purchase supports the design work that goes into every pattern.

Section 04

Returns & refunds

Because digital files are delivered instantly and cannot be returned, all sales of digital downloads are final. This is consistent with Etsy's standard policy for instant downloads and is the norm for digital products across all platforms.

Before purchasing, please review:

  • The size chart and finished measurements listed on the product page.
  • The skill level indicator — each pattern is rated for the sewist it suits.
  • The recommended fabrics list to make sure the design works with what you have.
  • The preview images showing a finished example and the pattern booklet cover.

If you receive a file that is corrupted, incomplete, or not what was described — please contact us immediately. We will resolve any genuine delivery issue without question.

Section 05

Having trouble with your download?

Most download issues have a simple fix. Try the following before reaching out:

  • Open the PDF using Adobe Acrobat Reader (free download) rather than your browser's built-in PDF viewer. Browser viewers sometimes strip layers or fail to render pattern pieces correctly.
  • If the download link has expired, log into your Etsy account and re-download from your Purchases page — the link is always active there.
  • Make sure you have enough storage space on your device before downloading.
  • On mobile, download to your Files app first, then open with a PDF reader app.

If none of the above works, please contact us through your Etsy order — we respond within 24 hours and will send you a working file directly.
